AI Art

Not so long ago, Humans believed that there was one thing a computer could never do better than a human and that was to create art. No we have reached a stage in society where computer generated art is becoming a norm.
Some people believe that AI-generated images are the end of art as we know it, and that they spell doom for the career of professional artists.
They believe that human made art will lose value and not be worth the time and effort when a computer can generate a work of art in a minute.
While AI may one day reach a stage of being able to produce art of higher quality than their human counterparts, I believe the current style of vague impressionist AI-generated art can be a great source of artistic inspiration for human artists,
whether they are painters or music producers or story writers.. AI art can spark the imagination and set the mood for a creative mind.
